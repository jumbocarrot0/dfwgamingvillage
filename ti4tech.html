Twilight Imperium 4: Technologies Reference

Green (Biotic) Technologies

Neural Motivator
During the status phase, draw 2 action cards instead of 1.
Prerequisite: None
Psychoarchaeology
Use tech specialties on planets you control without exhausting them. You can exhaust those planets for 1 trade good.
Prerequisite: None
Dacxive Animators
After you win a ground combat, you may place 1 infantry from reinforcements on that planet.
Prerequisite: 1 Green
Bio-Stims
Exhaust at the end of your turn to ready 1 planet with a tech specialty or 1 of your other technologies.
Prerequisite: 1 Green
Hyper Metabolism
During the status phase, gain 3 command tokens instead of 2.
Prerequisite: 2 Green
X-89 Bacterial Weapon Ω
After BOMBARDMENT, if at least 1 enemy infantry was destroyed, you may destroy all infantry on that planet.
Prerequisite: 3 Green

Blue (Propulsion) Technologies

Antimass Deflectors
Move through asteroid fields. Enemy Space Cannon rolls against your units are -1.
Prerequisite: None
Dark Energy Tap
Move through asteroid fields. Explore a planet without a sleeper token to place/move one.
Prerequisite: None
Gravity Drive
When activating a system, 1 ship gets +1 movement.
Prerequisite: 1 Blue
Sling Relay
ACTION: Exhaust to produce 1 ship at a space dock.
Prerequisite: 1 Blue
Fleet Logistics
You may perform 2 actions per turn instead of 1.
Prerequisite: 2 Blue
Light/Wave Deflector
Your ships can move through systems with other players’ ships.
Prerequisite: 3 Blue

Yellow (Cybernetic) Technologies

Sarween Tools
When you use Production, reduce the total cost by 1.
Prerequisite: None
Scanlink Drone Network
When activating a system, explore a planet in that system with your units.
Prerequisite: None
Graviton Laser System
Exhaust before a Space Cannon roll; hits must target non-fighter ships if able.
Prerequisite: 1 Yellow
Predictive Intelligence
During agenda phase, gain 3 votes. If your outcome fails, exhaust this. Can redistribute command tokens at end of turn.
Prerequisite: 1 Yellow
Transit Diodes
Exhaust to move up to 4 infantry to any planets you control.
Prerequisite: 2 Yellow
Integrated Economy
After gaining a planet, produce units on it equal to or less than its resources.
Prerequisite: 3 Yellow

Red (Warfare) Technologies

Plasma Scoring
When using Bombardment or Space Cannon, roll 1 extra die.
Prerequisite: None
AI Development Algorithm
When researching unit upgrades, ignore 1 prereq. During Production, reduce cost by your number of unit upgrades.
Prerequisite: None
Magen Defense Grid Ω
At start of ground combat on a planet with your structure, assign 1 hit to enemy ground forces.
Prerequisite: 1 Red
Self-Assembly Routines
After Production, you may place a mech. Gain 1 TG when one of your mechs is destroyed.
Prerequisite: 1 Red
Duranium Armor
After assigning hits in combat, repair 1 undamaged ship.
Prerequisite: 2 Red
Assault Cannon
At the start of space combat, if you have 3+ non-fighter ships, destroy 1 enemy non-fighter ship.
Prerequisite: 3 Red
